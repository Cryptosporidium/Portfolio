Snow Day

Using charcoal and pencil has been one of my favorite ways to draw. Always drawing things around daylight, I thought it would be more interesting to draw a snowy night.

A Hard Day's Work

This drawing was inspired by a story that I read long ago, where someone helping out on the farm would always spend time looking at the sunset. Sunsets create interesting shadows and lighting, so using charcoal to push and move the shadows in directions and make them harsher was a big challenge to get that full effect.
